Who am I?

In the past year, I moved to New Jersey as a young professional. Prior to that, I attended Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in Troy, NY where I studied Information Technology Web Science and Business Management. I grew up in northern VA, just outside of Washington, D.C. I love organizing anything and everything; its one of the few things that relaxes me after a long day at work. Most weekends you can find me hiking or just relaxing. In my free time I like to read, make lists, watch HGTV, play with my dog and listen to music.
